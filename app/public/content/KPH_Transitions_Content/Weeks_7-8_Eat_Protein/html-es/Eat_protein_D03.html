COMEN LA PROTEÍNA TEMPRANO Y ON FRECUENCIA

Get your protein from a variety of sources.

You can get protein from quite a few sources: animals (beef, pork, chicken, wild game) and fish, plants, dairy, nuts and seeds, and even some grains.

Here are some very simple suggestions.

  • Try a combination of proteins like beef, chicken, or pulled pork with beans, quinoa, and a slice of avocado over a bed of lettuce.
  • How about cottage cheese topped with smoked salmon?
  • Veggies have protein too. Add another serving of asparagus, artichoke, or broccoli to your dish or meal.
  • Lentils and beans contain some protein as well; however, you will need to pay particular attention to serving sizes and portions because you could easily consume more carbs than a balanced diet would allow.
