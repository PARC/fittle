COMEN LA PROTEÍNA TEMPRANO Y ON FRECUENCIA

Keep protein stocked at home or your place of work

  • Canned tuna, mackerel, albacore, sardines, salmon, chicken, anchovies
  • Low-sodium nitrate free turkey and chicken breast (whole or slices)
  • Eggs
  • Frozen shellfish (frozen or canned)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Canned beans, legumes
  • Dairy (cottage cheese, soft and hard cheeses, yogurt and milk) **
  • Grains (amaranth, quinoa, brown rice, sweet potato)
  • Oils (Walnut, Flax Seed, Olive Oil)

** Special Note: There are a quite a few people who have an inflammatory response to milk and dairy (including whey and casein proteins found in shakes and bars). Hopefully, you are well aware of any issues that you may have with dairy. As always, it is important to pay attention to YOUR body and how YOU respond to certain foods.
