Cooking Proteins

You can pretty much bake, poach, steam, broil, or grill your proteins.

Grilled chicken without the skin packs the most protein at about 28-35 grams for 3 ounces

Turkey breast is about the same although it tends to dry out pretty quickly, so it's a good idea to have a heart-healthy marinade to coat it with to keep it moist. Baking turkey breasts instead of grilling is another option.

Beef comes in at about 21 grams, while fish scores about 16 grams for those same 3 ounces.

HINT: When grilling proteins always use plenty of fresh rosemary on the coals to neutralize and counteract chemical compounds that are released when cooked at high temperatures. You can also sprinkle rosemary on top of the protein source, just make sure that it doesn't overpower or compete with other flavors.
