COMEN LA PROTEÍNA TEMPRANO Y ON FRECUENCIA

High-quality protein intake is key to a robust immune system and overall health

Today is the last day of the Eat Protein Early and Often habit.

If you were able to put some consistent effort in practicing this habit, you've likely had some great success with including proteins in your daily meal plan.

You might even think a bit differently about your protein options when putting together traditional meals and snacks, such as breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Key points to remember about the protein habit:

  • Eat your protein early in the day and throughout the day.
  • Get creative and explore various protein options. You can try anything once!
  • Re-invent some of your favorite protein-based recipes using a different protein.
  • Keep it lean and clean and choose from sustainable sources when possible.

Treat your proteins with the utmost respect for what they do for your body. They are the first-line of defense against bacteria and viruses and work in unison with white blood cells to boost our immunity.
