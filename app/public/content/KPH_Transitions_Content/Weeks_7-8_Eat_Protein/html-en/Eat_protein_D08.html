EAT PROTEIN EARLY AND OFTEN

What's for lunch or dinner?

Have an omelet with a small side salad, a frittata filled with your favorite veggies or a breakfast burrito with eggs, beans and salsa for dinner. Mix and match your proteins to your delight and keep exploring your options.

Don't be afraid to switch the order of breakfast, lunch, or dinner meals. Keep the spirit of experimentation and possibilities open. You just may find that a smaller portion of last night's dinner may just be the thing that starts the morning off right.
