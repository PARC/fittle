EAT PROTEIN EARLY AND OFTEN

Challenge your taste buds

Preparing your protein without all the added sauces and coatings and with healthier cooking methods, such as steaming instead of frying, will take some getting used to and may initially challenge your taste buds.

To stay motivated with this habit, keep in mind, that just like your mindset, your food preferences are in no way fixed. As you become a better cook and slowly re-introduce healthier versions of protein into your daily meal plan, your taste buds will adjust to new flavors and textures.

Tip: By consuming protein sources that are lean and clean (no sauces or coatings like bread crumbs or flour), you are likely to feel a bit lighter and more energetic, fuller on less food, and snack less throughout the day and evening.
