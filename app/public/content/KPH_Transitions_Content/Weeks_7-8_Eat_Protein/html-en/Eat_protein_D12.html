EAT PROTEIN EARLY AND OFTEN

Shopping for Proteins

Create a go-to customized shopping list. Shop for the freshest proteins, which are usually displayed around the perimeters of the grocery store.

Here's a short list to get you started.

  • Lean beef, pork, bison
  • Eggs (yolks and egg whites)
  • Fowl (chicken and turkey)
  • Fish
  • Pork
  • Cottage cheese
  • Tofu
  • Small Wild Fish
  • Wild/Troll-caught Canned Tuna

A few additional items to consider are:

  • Clams, mussels, scallops
  • Hard cheeses (Parmigiano-Reggiano, Parmesan)
  • Veggie burgers

Tip: Did you know that protein is a feel good food? It makes us feel calm and relaxed due to its hormone regulating properties, which keeps blood sugar levels steady.
