EAT PROTEIN EARLY AND OFTEN

Include more small wild fish in your diet

Akule/opelu (mackerel), sardines, anchovies, herring, and bluefish are very healthy options, loaded with Omega 3's (fatty acids) and unsaturated fat. If you're feeling adventurous try pickled herring.

Make this quick and tasty wild pickled herring recipe

Cut the herring into small pieces, drizzle with some olive oil and onions. For a quick snack, serve any of the small wild fish on a slice of toasted whole wheat, whole grain bread or crackers and you'll have a pretty darn healthy snack.

Tip: Protein is the ultimate fat burner. When we consume lean protein, it signals to the liver to release stored carbs and fat for energy. On the other hand, consuming highly processed and sugary carbs, grains, white flours, etc., signals to the body to pump out insulin which does the exact opposite: store new fat and prevents the release of carbs and fat for energy.
