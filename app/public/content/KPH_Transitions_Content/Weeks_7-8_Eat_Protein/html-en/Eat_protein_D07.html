EAT PROTEIN EARLY AND OFTEN

What do you consider a breakfast meal?

Today, challenge your mindset about what you consider appropriate breakfast foods and when you think you should eat certain proteins.

How about a salmon salad or a tofu stir-fry with asparagus and red bell peppers for breakfast? Many people are initially turned off by suggestions like these until they actually try it!

If you think about it, you've probably already changed many of your eating preferences without even realizing it. Go at your own pace, keep your mind open and be compassionate and patient with yourself.

Tip: Protein is the "Breakfast of Champions". Research has shown that by making protein the star of your morning meal, you will boost and stabilize your energy levels, feel fuller longer and experience fewer food cravings and insulin spikes.
