COMA MÁS LENTO

Make it a daily goal to eat at least one meal and snack slower than usual

If you are still struggling a bit with eating slowly and mindfully, try eating your meals and snacks to your favorite calming, soothing, and relaxing tune.

Select one or more tunes that will play longer than the time it normally takes for you to eat. Keep the volume reasonably low so that it does not become over-stimulating. It's a great way to train yourself to increase the time it takes to eat a meal, especially larger meals like dinner.
