Eating circumstances are really under your control

It is always possible to do this habit no matter what the environment or eating circumstances. Think about the office, home, a restaurant, celebrations, special events, etc.

Regardless of where you are, who’s there, the type of food being served, and when it is served. You can always practice this habit.

It's important to relax and understand that you cannot possibly control every aspect of an eating experience. Control what you can and call it a win!
