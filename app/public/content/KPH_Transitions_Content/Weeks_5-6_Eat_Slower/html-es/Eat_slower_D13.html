COMA MÁS LENTO

Try eating with fewer or no distractions today

Eliminate any of the following activities while you eat:

  • Using the computer
  • Texting
  • Watching any type of media
  • Talking on the phone

Completely tune in to the experience of eating no matter how slow you eat. If you are doing all the above, congratulate yourself! You are well on your way to becoming a most awesome mindful eater.
