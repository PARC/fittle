EAT SLOWER

Eat mindfully even when you are pressed for time and famished

If you are in a crunch when mealtime arrives, rather than rushing through meals and snacks, remain calm and slow down. You’ll have a better chance of reaching satiety, the feeling of fullness. Eat one-half of what you planned to eat and then move up your next mealtime a bit when you’re likely to have to more time.

Tip: It takes the brain a full 20 minutes to register that you've eaten something, so slow it down and let your body reach satiety.
