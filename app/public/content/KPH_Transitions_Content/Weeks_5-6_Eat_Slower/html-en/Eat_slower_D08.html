EAT SLOWER

Chew your food thoroughly

Today, notice the taste and texture of your food. Chew your food until it liquefies in your mouth. Take a deep breath between each bite of food.

Notice the effects of various foods on your digestive system and how it makes you feel after you've eaten them, especially after you have practiced eating slower. You might notice that some foods, usually the processed ones don't taste quite as good as you remember when you slow down and properly chew them.

Tip: If you are having issues with too much liquid in your mouth after chewing for several bites, try taking smaller bites. You should start to notice that you can chew another 10-15 times with less food in your mouth.
