POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

Stay inspired and motivated!!

Today, when you do anything positive, like eat mostly healthy meals/snacks, engage in some physical activity, or whatever, write it down on a small strip of paper, date it, and toss it into your "success" or "positive jar."

Your mini and micro successes are important too!

Visit your jar often and be thankful for how well you are doing. It can be motivating to remind yourself of just how successful you've been and how far you have come as you continue on your journey towards better health.

Tip: See a medical professional for a quick check-up. Make an appointment with your doctor or dentist to deal with any aggravating health issues that you've been either ignoring or neglecting.
