POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

Practicing small changes consistently leads to significant changes down the road

Team Activity

Share with the team today: What new healthy items will you try this week?

Change is not always easy, and it often feels like it isn't happening fast enough, but with a little patience, focus, and motivation to change, anything is possible.

Focus on making a little progress each and every day and let go of the notion of the need to be perfect.

Do one small action that aligns with your long-term heal goals. For example, you could:

  • Plan a few meals or snacks in advance
  • Place your workout clothes on your dresser the night before
  • Leave your workout shoes by the front door.

Tip: It is only through the act of doing and doing something consistently that you can expect to see any real lasting change in your behaviors and habits.
