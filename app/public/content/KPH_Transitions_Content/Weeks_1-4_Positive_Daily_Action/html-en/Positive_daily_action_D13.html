POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

Underestimating calories

Research suggests that people on a weight-loss program underestimate how much they eat each day by approximately 25-40%.

Sit-down restaurants are less accurate than fast food restaurants in reporting the caloric content of menu items listed for 625 calories or less, which is not good news for anyone trying to make a serious effort to choose healthier foods and reduce their weight.

Record all of those nibbles and bites that manage to sneak their way into your daily food intake. If you believe a taste here and there is not thwarting your weight reduction goals, think again!

Tip: It's been reported that the caloric cost of sampling at Costco could easily reach a whopping 943 calories!
