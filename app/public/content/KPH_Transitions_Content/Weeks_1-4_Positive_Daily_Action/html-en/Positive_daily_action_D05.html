POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

What's important to you?


Team Activity

Share with the team your top five priorities and rank them in order of importance.

Where does YOUR health appear on the list?

Note if you are devoting the necessary time and energy to those priorities as your ranking suggests. If there isn't a pretty good match, commit to the team to take action today.

Re-focus your efforts on your top priorities

Take a moment to re-focus and give some serious thought about your priorities. On the surface, many of us believe that our health is a top priority. But, if we are willing to dig a little deeper, we often realize that our actions and behaviors do not match up to the level of importance as suggested by our ranking.

If you feel that you could do a little more to match your behaviors to your desired goal of better health, what one small thing could you do right now to make that happen? What do you need?


Tip: One small, doable action to closer align your behaviors and actions to your health goals is to compile a quick list of healthy items that you plan to try this upcoming week. You'll be taking positive action toward your health goals.
