POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

Focus on any one thing that's important to you

Your only goal with this habit is to focus and dedicate 5, 10 or 15-minutes each day on something that you truly want to improve or change.

Can't decide? It's okay. Spend 5 or 10-minutes thinking about a few ideas or tasks that are super important to you, break it down into small, actionable items and write them down.

Now identify the actionable items that you feel you could execute with 90% certainty and do it today!

If you are part of the Kaiser-Permanente Hawaii Program and you are feeling a bit overwhelmed or can't decide what to choose for a daily action, remember completion of any classroom activities or homework count towards the habit and goal of daily change.
