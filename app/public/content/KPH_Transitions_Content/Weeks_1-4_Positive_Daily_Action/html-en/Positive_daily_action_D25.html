POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

Choose long-term stress reduction strategies that will help, not harm your health

First, let's state the obvious: Daily stressors and stress, in general, are just part of life, so we must inevitably learn how to deal with them and develop health enhancing strategies that help our health, not harm it.

Using food to soothe ourselves when we are overwhelmed or stressed out is never a long-term solution, and it certainly hurts our health in the long run.

If you are interested in doing more to reduce your stress levels right now, check out this list of 5, 10, or 15-minute stress reduction actions that you can do today!
  • Schedule a few short walks throughout the day. A 5 to 10-minute walk in the middle of the day will do wonders for your head and heart.
  • Take a 10-minute walk after dinner and encourage the whole family to participate. Commit to doing this at least once a week until it becomes routine on most evenings.
  • Take a 15-minute candlelit bath once a week and pack your running shoes or workout clothes for the next day while you wait for the tub to fill up. Make a point of scheduling your baths on the days that tend to be more stressful. Carve out those most important 15-minutes and keep the appointment with yourself!

Tip: Keep bringing your attention back to the positive aspects of your personal journey of health and fitness. Your goals and destination point will slowly come into clearer view.
