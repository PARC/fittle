POSITIVE DAILY ACTION

What's your motivation?

Think back to the time before you started your weight reduction program.

Why did you start the program and what was your motivation? Previous participants in the program gave these reasons:

  • Look and feel better
  • Be healthy for my kids
  • Live a happier and long life

Do any of these resonate with you?

Can you think of other reasons to stay positive and motivated?

When you are thinking about what motivates you, try to define what you want (rather than what you don't want) because it's a lot easier to take action when you do. For example, "look and feel better" could be further defined as, "look and feel better"(with strong bones and muscles).

If you are a bit stuck, take a few moments and think about what could you do right NOW to improve or re-focus the trajectory of your motivation to stay on track.

Tip: Starting today, put motivational reminders everywhere possible to keep your particular motivation for change fresh and in the forefront of your mind.
