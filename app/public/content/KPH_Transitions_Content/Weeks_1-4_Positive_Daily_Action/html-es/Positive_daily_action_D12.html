ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Food journals are just objective data

If you are currently keeping a food journal, spend 5 to 15-minutes reviewing it and look for patterns and tendencies.

For example, are you prone to eat more prepackaged and processed foods during certain times of the day or specific days of the week?

Mid-afternoon and late night can be very challenging periods of the day when trying to stay on track with a healthier eating plan.

Overeating on the weekends is a problem for many us, but that's more of social habit than anything else.

What can you do to disrupt a pattern or routine that promotes an undesirable behavior surrounding food?

What one small action could you do right NOW to make snacks or meals just a bit healthier?

Tip: Prep healthier food options in advance and keep them readily available when you feel urges or cravings coming on and eat them before you consume your usual choice of foods.
