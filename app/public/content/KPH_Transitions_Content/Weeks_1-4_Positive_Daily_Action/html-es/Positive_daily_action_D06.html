Dining out strategies can help you to stay on track

Consider modifying or combining one or more dining out strategies to help you stay on track and a bit more in control of your eating during social events, celebrations, and holidays.

Here's how to get started

Step 1 - Gather some information

What kind of food will be served at the event? Will it be a prix fixe menu, banquet, or buffet style? Hors d'oeuvres, small appetizers, or packaged snack size foods?

Step 2 - Craft a Plan

  1. Review online restaurant menus in advance and decide what substitutions or requests you'll need to make to bring your meals more in line with your new style of eating.
  2. Plan a visit to the restaurant before the occasion and review the menu in person. Try to get a feel for the place and what the atmosphere might be like when you are dining there.
  3. Make a sincere effort to reach out to someone who might have an idea about what kind of food will likely be served at the event.
