ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Be aware and observant of your natural response to change and what your inner voice communicates to you

Many people struggle with thoughts and feelings from their inner voice like, "If I eat out I will get carried away and abandon my eating plan" or "I'm an emotional eater, and if anyone or anything causes me to feel uncomfortable or upset, I will overeat just like always."

If any of this sounds familiar, be patient and observant of this inner voice. It is nothing more than a string of well-rehearsed thoughts about something or a situation, and you are simply having an emotional reaction to them.

These types of thoughts will come and go and do not determine who you are as a person. You don't have to allow this kind of "negative self-talk" to derail your best efforts and cause you to lose your focus and motivation.
