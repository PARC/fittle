ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Willpower will only get you so far; how resilient you are matters the most.

Real positive change is not sustained from sheer willpower, especially if you view willpower as a finite resource. Lasting change takes focus and resilience, which is simply your ability to recover and move on from mistakes or difficulties.

If you don't automatically view yourself as being a resilient individual, take a few moments and think about some tough times or experiences in your life.

Chances are, there are at least half-a-dozen or more occasions where you triumphed over incredible odds, obstacles, problems, and persevered even when you just didn't think you could go any further.

Tip: Write down three major experiences in your life that you could use as motivation and a testament to your resilience. Toss it into your Positive Jar or post it somewhere where you can see it often.
