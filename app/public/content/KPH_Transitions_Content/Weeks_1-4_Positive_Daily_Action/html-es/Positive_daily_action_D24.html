ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Quick positive daily actions that can help reduce stress

  • Drink one glass of water upon waking before your morning coffee or tea. Headaches, migraines, depression, shoulder and neck pain, mood swings, low energy levels, and impaired cognition are all linked to mild dehydration
  • Drink unsweetened green tea instead of coffee in the afternoon to increase antioxidants and polyphenols in your diet
  • Go to bed 1/2 hour earlier than normal and wake up 1/2 hour earlier. Use that time to prep some food for snacks, lunch, or dinner. Or plan your schedule for the next few days.
  • Spend 5 to 10-minutes each day writing in your journal about anything positive that happened during the day
  • Sign up for one or more swimming or dance classes
  • Visit your neighborhood gym and ask for a tour or look at some trail maps for walking and hiking routes near you and plan a hike today!

Tip: Choose any one of these actions and get back on track today, if you need to, or try something new to keep your practice of daily change going strong.
