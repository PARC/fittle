Food journals, are they really that important?

Team Activity

What does your healthy plate look like? Take a pic and share with the team.

Keeping a food journal is extremely useful when trying to determine where improvements can be made in your nutritional knowledge and habits.

Provide as much detail about, (a) what your meals consist of, (b) when you eat them, and (c) anything else about the meal that you feel is important.

Tip: Food journals can reveal crucial information about your eating behaviors. Look for patterns in your eating behaviors such as time, location, and your physical and emotional state.
