ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Do your nutritional strategies need a little more attention or improvement?

Team Activity

Share with the team any quick, effective, small, and doable action(s) that has/have led to an improvement in your Dining Out, Meal Planning, Living to Thrive, or Stress Management practice and strategies.

For any strategy that is not working so well, first determine how and if you need to break it down into smaller chunks. Then decide what "one" small thing could you do to reinforce the strategy to make it work a tiny bit better.

If you need help, ask your teammates.
