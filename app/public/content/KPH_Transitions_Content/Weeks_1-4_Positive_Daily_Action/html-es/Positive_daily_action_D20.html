ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Some progress is better than no progress; things are always changing

"Even if you fall on your face, you are still moving forward." ~Victor Klam.

You may not immediately recognize or realize progress if you are viewing your actions through lenses that tend to judge events or life as either good or bad.

Try to remain neutral for one day and refrain from judging people, places, and events, including yourself. This little and powerful exercise may leave you feeling just a bit more positive and peaceful at the end of the day.

Certainly, everything in your life will not change overnight, but it will give you some context for the inevitability of having to "start over", "clean the slate," or "hit the reset button,"' which are all necessary in your quest for meaningful change.
