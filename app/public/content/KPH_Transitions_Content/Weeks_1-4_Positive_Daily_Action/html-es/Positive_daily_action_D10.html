ACCIÓN DIARIA POSITIVA

Commit to some type of daily physical action

Consider making physical activity a daily positive action. In fact, the release of endorphins, the feel-good chemicals in the brain that are mainly released by the central nervous system, will help you to relax, calm your nerves, and boost your spirits. It might even make focusing on those nutrition habits a bit easier.

Action Ideas

You could repeat the same action a few days in a row and say something like,
  • "Today and tomorrow, I will stretch for five minutes or take a 5-minute walk."

Or you could do an entirely new task every day

  • "Today I will take the stairs, and tomorrow, I will add 1/4 cup of veggies to a particular meal."

Either method you use is a step in the right direction, and you are priming your brain for positive habit formation and change.


Tip: Daily action or physical movement is the catalyst for positive change. Once you get going, there is nothing more motivating or inspiring than witnessing your own body in motion.
