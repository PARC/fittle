Increase your vegetable intake

You may not be quite ready to eat vegetables with every meal, but you could certainly start by snacking on baby carrots or trying celery stalks with a few tablespoons of hummus, etc.

Choose high-fiber veggies such as:
  • Bell peppers (green, red, or yellow)
  • Cucumber
  • Fennel
  • Zucchini
  • Green beans
  • Snow peas
  • Chickpeas/Garbanzo beans
