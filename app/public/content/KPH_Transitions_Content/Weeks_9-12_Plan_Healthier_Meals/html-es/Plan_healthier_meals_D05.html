The Farmer's Market Rules!

If you haven't had the pleasure of shopping at a farmer's market, you are missing out on an awesome experience that will make you feel more connected to your food. Look for a Hawaii Farmers Market near you and buy a few days worth of produce for the week!

Take the family or friends and make it outing on your visit to the Farmer's Market. Buy items that are locally grown and currently in season from the Hawaiian Seasonal Fruits and Vegetable list.
