PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Cook quick and easy meals:

  • Be mindful about the amount of time that you can honestly dedicate to actually prepping and cooking meals. If all you have is 15 minutes, then that's great! That is your starting point. Your skills will only become much more effective and efficient over time.
  • Start small and keep it simple. If you are new to cooking, or your cooking skills need some polishing up, now is probably not the time to try your hand at creating complicated and elaborate dishes.
  • Limit your recipes to as few common and flavorful ingredients possible, unless of course, you really enjoy cooking and have the time.
Tip: Many people have experienced “cooking burnout” within the first week of practicing this habit due to just one poorly ill-conceived, over the top, executed dish. Don't be one of those persons. Keep it simple.
