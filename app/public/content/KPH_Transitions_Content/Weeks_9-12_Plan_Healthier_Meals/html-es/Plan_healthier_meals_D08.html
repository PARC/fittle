PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Eat complete and lean protein with each meal

Make protein the "star" of your meals, followed by veggies, a serving of starchy carbs, and finally, a small portion of healthy fats.

Eat a boiled egg, fish (canned sardines, tuna in water or frozen seafood), fowl (chicken, turkey) or lean cuts of meat. Try some cooked lentils or legumes and brown rice. Make sure you adjust your serving size and portions when using veggies and starches to meet your daily protein goals.

For Vegetarians

If you are a vegetarian or vegan, you may need to spend a little extra time and effort on figuring-out various combinations of foods to meet your daily protein goals while balancing your carb load. You will also want to make sure that you are getting enough complete proteins in your diet. Need some help? Check out the 10 Complete Proteins for Vegetarians.
