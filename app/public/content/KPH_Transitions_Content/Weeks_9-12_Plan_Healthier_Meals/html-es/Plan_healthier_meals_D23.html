PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Shopping day

A few tips and reminders about shopping and grocery store layouts:

  • Shop around the perimeters of the store, with the exception of the bakery and deli section. You can find proteins, fruits, and veggies there.
  • Avoid the middle aisles where the canned goods, frozen and processed foods, chips, and beverages can be found.
  • Never buy anything from a display case at the front or end of an isle.
  • Never shop when you are hungry, wander around the store looking for your next meal, or attempt to plan meals on the fly.
  • Resist the urge to purchase impulse items near, at, or around the cash register.
Tip: KNOW BEFORE YOU GO! Make sure you know what you're buying when you enter the grocery store. The longer you linger in the store, the less likely you will walk out of the store with healthy items.
