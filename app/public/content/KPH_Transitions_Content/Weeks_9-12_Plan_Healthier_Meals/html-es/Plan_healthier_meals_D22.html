PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Keep adding a variety of healthy foods to your daily meal plans to avoid becoming bored

To stave off boredom due to eating the same meals over and over again, once or twice a week try something new. Notice if the food on your plate represents the colors of the rainbow or does everything appear to be the same color and texture?

If you need to spruce things up a bit, a great place to start is the farmer's market. Ask questions about unusual foods that you see or perhaps, a specific food that piques your interest.

Tip: Occasionally, take pictures of your meals and snacks. If your meals are lackluster, think of one or two foods that are vibrant with color.
