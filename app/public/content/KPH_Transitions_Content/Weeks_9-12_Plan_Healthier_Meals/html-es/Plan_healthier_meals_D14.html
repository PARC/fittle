PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Eat some healthy fats

Omega 3's

Strive to consume all three types of fat: saturated, monounsaturated, and polyunsaturated. Keep ratios to about 1/3 each.

  • Saturated fats should come from animal protein or coconut oil and small amounts of butter from the milk of grass-fed cows.
  • Monounsaturated fats should come from mixed nuts, olives, avocados, and high-quality extra virgin olive oil.
  • Polyunsaturated fats should come from flax seeds, fish or algae oil, and mixed nuts.

Omega 6's

Most people consume far too much Omega 6's in their diet. Research has shown that consuming these types of fats in excess harms heart health, increases cancer risk, and inflammatory and autoimmune diseases when not consumed in balance with other fats and oils.

A short list of culprits to watch out for are:
  • Processed foods (chips, cookies, cakes, pastries, pies)
  • Fast food
  • Margarine
  • Shortening
  • Vegetable oil
  • Corn oil
  • Soy oil

*Avoid cooking with canola oil. It becomes unstable when exposed to heat and creates trans fatty acids (trans fats) and free radicals.
