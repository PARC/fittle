PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

Keep healthier carbs options readily available.

Along the spectrum of carbs to choose from, the following options are much better choices when planning heart-healthy meals.

  • Whole-wheat flour
  • Whole-wheat pasta
  • Whole-wheat and Whole grain bread
  • Steel-cut oatmeal
  • Rice (brown and wild)
  • Quinoa, Bulgar, Barley, Couscous, Kaniwa
  • Potatoes (Purple, Red, Yukon Gold)
