PLANIFIQUE COMIDAS SALUDABLES

The fourth habit of the Thrive with Real Foods Challenge is to Plan Healthier Meals. By now, you may be getting the sense that the most important aspect of learning any new habit is to start small and keep it SUPER simple. Especially as you learn how to build and incorporate healthier meals into your daily eating plan.

How The Habit Works

Your goal is to plan and prep as many balanced and compliant meals in advance based on your unique personal dietary needs.

You can accomplish this by either setting aside one day to plan, shop, prep, and cook some meals for the entire week. Or you may choose to devote some time every 2-3 days breaking up the various tasks however you prefer by preparing 5 to 6 meals, including snacks, at a time.

Gradually, you will figure out what works best for you and your schedule.

How to practice this habit and make it work for you

However you decide to practice this habit, it will require that you devote a block of time to focus and plan what you'll eat and when you will prepare it.

Healthy meals do not magically come together, although we all wished that they did, which is why it is important not to worry or become overly anxious about practicing this habit. You will be introduced to some very basic and pertinent skills that will compliment and support your practice of planning healthier meals consistently, as well as controlling portion sizes.

General rules and guidelines

Each day you will be given some general rules and guidance aimed at keeping you focused and engaged in this process while you progress toward the goal of successfully planning healthier meals consistently.

What can I expect to learn after practicing this habit?

Ideally, at the end of practicing this habit, you will have gained greater confidence in your ability to plan your meals and become a slightly better cook. You may even be surprised by how much more content and satisfied you are with eating self-prepared meals and snacks.

One last cool thing about this habit is that it is a major confidence booster, as you acquire new strategies and an improved skill set that will aid you in successfully managing your weight in the long run.

Today, spend 5 to 10 minutes planning some meals and snacks to earn your mark for the day.
