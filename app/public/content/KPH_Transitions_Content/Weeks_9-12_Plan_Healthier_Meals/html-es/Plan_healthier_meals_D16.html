Sugary Drinks

Eventually you will want to phase out fruit juices or any other sweetened calorie-laden beverages. They literally bring no nutritional value to your ideal healthy plate. You can start right now by diluting any beverage with water.

Tip: Like the taste of sparkly water? Invest in a Sodastream and make your own healthy soda pop with infused ingredients like, mint, ginger, basil, lemons, oranges, limes, etc. The possibilities are endless!
