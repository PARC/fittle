Check your kitchen tools

Make sure that you have plenty of proper storage containers. Some are quite useful for measuring and portion control. For example, if you know that a particular container holds a pint of food then by all means use it!

Glass and ceramic are ideal for refrigerating and freezing prepped and precooked foods. Making meals super easy to heat up in a flash and grab on the go.

Tip: If you don't already own one, consider purchasing a good roasting pan. Try your hand at roasting up a variety of vegetables. Just add some of your favorite seasonings, a little avocado oil for cooking at high heat, and some low-sodium chicken or vegetable stock to prevent burning and you've got delicious roasted veggies ready to go.
