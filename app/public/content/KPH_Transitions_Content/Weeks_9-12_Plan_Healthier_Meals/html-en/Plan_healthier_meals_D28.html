PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

Now you have some awesome skills to plan healthier meals.

Today is the last day of the Plan Healthier Meals habit. By sticking with this habit for an entire month, there's no doubt you will have acquired at least one other useful skill set.

Recap of some basic skills for planning healthier meals:

Gradually, planning, shopping, and prepping your meals will get a little easier and a lot less intimidating.
  1. Allocate some time each week for planning, shopping, prepping, and cooking meals a few meals at a time or the entire week.
  2. Learn some basic cooking skills. At the very least, learn how to warm things up.
  3. Take your shopping list with you each week to prevent wandering around the store. Do not ever shop hungry.
  4. Stick to the perimeters of the grocery store when shopping.
  5. Don’t believe the hype. Remember most foods that make "health" claims are probably not all that healthy for you.
  6. Avoid most pre-packaged foods whenever possible because they tend to be highly processed and do not even come close to resembling the origin of the whole food.
  7. If you buy pre-packaged foods try to buy foods that have no more than 7 ingredients listed on the package.

Tip: Before deciding to purchase any food item, ask yourself two simple questions, "Is this the best possible choice I can make right now, and will this particular food item deliver the nourishment my body needs in order to function and perform at its best?
