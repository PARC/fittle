PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

Repeat Team Activity

If you didn't get a chance to do yesterday's group activity do it today, or repeat it if you have the time

Prepare a 15-minute meal with only 7 to 10 common ingredients.

  • Take a picture and share* with the team
  • Comment or applaud aesthetically pleasing meals
  • After everyone shares, your team should vote for the overall winning meal made with the fewest ingredients.
  • Have fun eating healthfully!
*It would also be helpful sharing your recipe with the team and what the experience was like for you.
