PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

Eat at least two servings of fruit each day

Try different types of berries (blackberries, gooseberries, or raspberries). They're loaded with several significant anti-oxidant properties.

Other fruits such as apples, pears, or plums are great choices too.

Have you ever tried a "carambola" or better known as, "starfruit?" The smaller version is less sweet and a bit more tart. Both are high in potassium and vitamin C and low in sugar.
