PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

What's your food preparation system?

Creating a food prep system is not as intimidating or as complicated as it seems. With some consistent practice, you will become so efficient with prepping food that cooking will be a breeze.

Easy how-to create your own food prep system

At a minimum, here are a few things you'll need to learn how to do to get your own system up and running:

  • Chop up veggies for salads, omelets, frittata's, soups, and stews or for munching on healthy snacks.
  • Precook some proteins or keep canned small wild fish in stock, deli turkey (without the nitrates) or smoked wild salmon in your fridge.
  • Batch cook beans, stews, and soups or create your own version of a healthy "multi-meal" made with protein, veggies and some starchy carbs. Freeze and enjoy them later or add them to other recipes.

Tip: Keep some organic, low-sodium, canned tomatoes, chicken, vegetable stock, and sauces with no added sugar stocked in your pantry. Start with any one of these as a base, toss in some chopped veggies, add some protein and - voila! You've got a stew or soup in minutes.
