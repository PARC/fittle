PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

Eat every 3-5 hours

If you can't manage to eat something every 3 to 5 hours, then aim to decrease the amount of time between your current meals.

For example, if you usually eat every 6 to 7 hours cut it down to every 5 hours or less. Eating at least three meals a day (about 200 to 500 kcal) is easier on the digestive system than eating one huge meal.

You should notice that your mood and energy levels are a bit more stabilized throughout the day, providing your brain power with a much- needed boost it needs to function at its optimal capacity.
