Practice and perfect your portion control skills

Use a food scale, measuring cups, and spoons. Or practice using an eyeball test for controlling portions. The most helpful pictures starts on slide 13.

You could also use your hand as a nifty measuring tool. See use your hand as a guide to portion control. (If you use this method of measuring food, men should double the portions shown on the chart.

If you are unsure of your best guesstimation for a desired portion of a specific food or meal, take a picture and share it with the team to get some feedback.

Tip: Most varieties of fruits and veggies come in small, medium, and large sizes. Try not to over think your portion sizes, but pay close attention to them.
