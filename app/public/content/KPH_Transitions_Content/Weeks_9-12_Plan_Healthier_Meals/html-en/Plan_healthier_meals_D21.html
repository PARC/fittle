PLAN HEALTHIER MEALS

Slowly develop your food preparation skills and strategies

One of the easiest food preparation strategies to master is the habit of preparing healthy snacks in advance and keep them where you can easily get to them.

Knowing what to eat is great, but it's not very helpful if the food item isn't available when you need it. You'll likely choose something that's convenient,less healthy, and risk staying on course with your eating plan.

Simple snack ideas

  • Protein: cottage cheese, hard-boiled egg, hummus
  • Veggies: eat them raw
  • Starchy Carbs: whole grain and whole wheat. Be mindful of proper portion sizes
  • Eat a serving of nuts or have some nut butter. Watch out if you can't stop at just one serving
Tip: While you're waiting for something to cook or heat up, why not make a few healthy snacks in advance?
