Mastering Habits - Day 3

Share with the team those habits that you have (a) either mastered, (b) had a major break-through, or (c) saw a great improvement in the frequency in which you practice the habit. Be sure to consider the following questions when sharing with your teammates:
  1. What conditions existed or did you have to create in order for you to master the habit?
  2. What's the most important thing(s) you currently do to maintain mastery of the habit?
  3. How do you plan to continue building on the success of your current habits and future habits?
  4. Why is the mastery of this habit important to you?

Examples of improvements in desired behaviors

Continue thinking about the planning healthier meals habit. Have you noticed any changes or improvement in the examples below?
  • Do you feel much more confident in your ability to plan meals on the fly or with limited ingredients?
  • Are you a little less panicky at the mere thought of having to whip up a compliant and delicious meal in just 15-minutes?
  • When you are considering recipes to try you no longer become overwhelmed and moved to inaction by the sheer amount of options available. Your immediate reaction is "how can I make it compliant, do I have enough time, and how many ingredients will I need to substitute or hunt down to make it"?

Please note that these are only suggested topics. Feel free to discuss any habit that you'd like and share with your teammates and why it's important to you!
