Mastering Habits - Day 4

Make another posting reflecting on those habits that you (a) have either mastered, (b) had a major break-through, or (c) saw a great improvement in the frequency in which you practice the habit.

Be sure to consider the following questions when sharing with your teammates:

  1. What conditions existed or did you have to create in order for you to master the habit?
  2. What's the most important thing(s) you currently do to maintain mastery of the habit?
  3. How do you plan to continue building on the success of your current habits and future habits?
  4. Why is the mastery of this habit important to you?

Mastery of habits examples from the Eating Protein Early & Often habit

  • Finding ways to add some protein to every meal is getting a bit easier because you stock, shop, plan, and prep accordingly
  • You feel stronger and stand a bit taller. You're even starting to notice that some muscles are taking shape, and some are making their first appearance ever!
  • Maybe you're getting a little less sick than you used to, thanks to a healthier immune system

Please note that these are only suggested topics. Feel free to discuss any habit that you'd like and share with your teammates and why it's important to you!
