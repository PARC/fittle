Successful behavior goal examples

Another way to think about successful behavior goals as you may have experienced them during the Thrive with Real Foods Challenge are listed below. Many of these subtle behaviors are huge wins in the process of positive change. Do any of them resonate with you?

  • You took action consistently and regularly
    • Drank water whenever you went walking or to the gym
  • You managed small goals and any related small tasks that were under your control
    • Prepped three meals and three snacks at a time
  • You took swift, immediate action
    • Practiced your 5 to 15-minute daily action whenever the opportunity arose
  • Took action later in the day
    • Reported your lesson and habit in Fittle as complete
  • Took action the next day
    • Caught up on your workouts
  • Took action as soon as it was feasible
    • Purchased a new cooking utensil or cookware to reduce the amount of time spent in the kitchen

Please note that the examples presented here are only suggestions. Feel free to discuss any habit that you'd like and share with your teammates.
