How do you measure progress?

Team Activity

Share with the team any positive changes that you've noticed that are a little different than the standard diet and exercise outcome goals that focus on pounds and inches.

Examples of other ways to measure progress

There are dozens of ways to measure progress other than pounds, inches, pant and dress size. Take a look at a few examples below:

  • How do you feel? Are you more energetic? More positive?
  • Have you noticed any aches or pains that have suddenly disappeared?
  • Do you feel a little less self-conscious or slightly more comfortable in your own skin?
  • Have you seen an improvement in your blood work?
  • Have you reduced or eliminated certain medications?
