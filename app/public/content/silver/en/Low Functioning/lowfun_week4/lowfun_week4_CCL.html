Lower Body: Standing Heel Raises

This exercise is to strengthen the muscles in your lower leg. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Equipment:

Sturdy chair (or can perform at counter top for more stability)

*Note- You will feel the muscles in the back of your lower leg working.  

Instructions:

  1. Stand tall holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Raise your heels up, rolling onto the balls of your feet.
  3. Slowly return your heels back to the ground.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Making it harder (more challenging):

  1. Stand tall holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Stand on one foot.
  3. On your standing leg, raise your heel up, rolling onto the ball of your foot.
  4. Slowly return your heel back to the ground.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Making it easier (less challenging):

  1. Sit tall in a chair.
  2. Raise your heels up, rolling onto the balls of your feet.
  3. Slowly return your heels back to the ground.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Lower Body: Leg Exercise- Side

This exercise is to strengthen the muscles on the side of your hip and improve your balance. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Equipment:

Sturdy chair (or can perform at counter top for more stability)

*Note- You will feel the muscles in your hip working on the both the standing leg and the moving leg.  

Instructions:

  1. Stand tall holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Raise one leg to the side, then slowly lower the leg back to starting.
  3. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Making it harder (more challenging):

  1. Stand tall next to the chair or counter.
  2. Put your hands on your hips.
  3. Raise one leg to the side, then slowly lower the leg back to starting.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Making it easier (less challenging):

  1. Stand tall holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Slide one leg out to the side with minimal pressure on the ground for support.
  3. Slowly lower the leg back to starting.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

Lower Body: Supported Squat

This exercise is to strengthen the muscles in your hips and legs. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Equipment:

Sturdy chair (or can perform at counter top for more stability), weighted object (ie: half gallon of water, progression only)

*Note- You will feel the muscles in your buttocks and front and back of thighs working.  

Instructions:

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip width apart holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Slowly lower your hips down as if you are trying to sit in a chair.
  3. Slowly return to standing.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Making it harder (more challenging):

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip width apart holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. With one hand hold a weighted object (ie: half gallon of water).
  3. Slowly lower your hips down as if you are trying to sit in a chair.
  4. Slowly return to standing.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Making it easier (less challenging):

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip width apart holding on to the chair or counter.
  2. Slowly lower your hips down as if you are trying to sit in a chair.
  3. Only lower about halfway.
  4. Slowly return to standing.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.
