Lower Body: Hamstring Exercise

This exercise is to strengthen the muscles in your thigh. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

 

Equipment:

 

Sturdy chair, theraband (more challenging only)

 

*Note- You will feel the muscles in the back of your thigh working.

 

Instructions:

 

  1. Begin standing behind the chair, holding the backrest for support.
  2. Curl one foot towards your buttocks as much as can.
  3. Return foot to ground.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

 

Making it harder (more challenging):

 

  1. Tie your theraband to the front leg of the chair.
  2. Stand behind the chair and loop your foot through the theraband, holding the backrest for support.
  3. Curl one foot towards your buttocks.
  4. Return foot to ground.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

 

Making it easier (less challenging):

 

  1. Begin standing behind the chair, holding the backrest for support.
  2. Curl one foot towards your buttocks about 6 inches off the ground.
  3. Return foot to ground.
  4. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions on each leg.

 Flexibility: Spine Exercise

This exercise is to strengthen the muscles in your trunk (your abdomen and spine). Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions of this exercise.

Equipment:

Sturdy chair, a short cup 4-6 inches in height, a soup can (more challenging only), tall cup 8-10 inches in height (less challenging only)

*Note- You will feel the muscles on the sides of your trunk working.

Instructions:

  1. Place a short cup on the floor to one side of the chair.
  2. Start seated tall in the chair.
  3. Without twisting, lean to the side, grab the cup and return to sitting tall.
  4. Lean to the opposite side, placing the cup on the ground and return to sitting.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Making it harder (more challenging):

  1. Place a soup can on the floor to one side of the chair.
  2. Start seated tall in the chair.
  3. Without twisting, lean to the side, grab the can and return to sitting tall.
  4. Lean to the opposite side, placing the can on the ground and return to sitting.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Making it easier (less challenging):

  1. Place a tall cup on the floor to one side of the chair.
  2. Start seated tall in the chair.
  3. Without twisting, lean to the side, grab the cup and return to sitting tall.
  4. Lean to the opposite side, placing the cup on the ground and return to sitting.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

Lower Body: Bridge

This exercise is the strengthen the muscles in your legs and buttocks. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

 

Equipment:

 

Flat surface (your bed or floor), heavy book (more challenging only)

 

*Note: you feel the muscles in your buttocks and back of your legs working.

 

Instructions:

 

  1. Begin laying flat on your back with your knees bent, feet placed under your knees.
  2. Rest your arms long by your side.
  3. Push through your heels, to lift your hips up until your trunk is in a straight line.
  4. Return your hips back to the surface.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

 

Making it harder (more challenging):

 

  1. Begin laying flat on your back with your knees bent, feet placed under your knees.
  2. Hold a heavy book to the front of your hips.
  3. Push through your heels, to lift your hips up until your trunk is in a straight line.
  4. Return your hips back to the surface.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.

 

Making it easier (less challenging):

 

  1. Begin laying flat on your back with your knees bent, feet placed under your knees.
  2. Rest your arms long by your side.
  3. Push through your heels, to lift your hips up 2-4 inches off the surface.
  4. Return your hips back to the surface.
  5. This is one repetition. Remember you need to do 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.
